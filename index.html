It was Okay...Not Quite What I Wanted.

Being a fan of the 1984 adaptation, I didn't love this new 'modern' version. The cast was good, however it just didn't have enough of the essence of the book world that I was looking for.

Improvement, but doesn't quite hit the mark.

So, from the outset, this film can't help but be better than the 1984 version. BUT, it manages to do something I didn't expect...it's actually TOO long...or so it felt due to the pacing and ridiculous number of big scenes were not much happens but you see a lot of eye candy...along with very moody music. In many ways, it reminded me of the first "Star Trek" movie...which was so long and tedious.

Steve M.
